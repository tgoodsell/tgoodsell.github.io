Tuition and Aid

At Kepler, you have the opportunity to pay in full up front (and get a 10% discount on tuition when paid in a year) , to pay as you study, or to select a 100% deferred tuition option. For the upcoming academic year, the cost of studying at Kepler is as follows:
  • Annual tuition: 800,000 RWF
  • Annual housing: 675,000 RWF*
  • Annual stipend: 324,000 RWF*
In addition, all students who are accepted and choose to enroll at Kepler will be required to pay a 50,000 RWF registration fee to secure their spot. This will be communicated to students during the acceptance process and is usually due in mid-May.

*Students may opt out of Kepler housing after the first year of study.
*Students may opt out of stipend after the first year of study

Apply to join the Kepler community today. Kepler leverages a unique, blended curriculum offering innovative online learning paired with in-person coaching to deliver the skills needed to succeed in today’s global economy, all while working toward a U.S.-accredited bachelor’s degree. We’re looking for talented students of all backgrounds with demonstrated leadership skills and an interest in business studies to join our growing community of future leaders.

Admission Criteria

  • Strong interest in business studies
  • Strong English skills
  • High school scores (last two years)
  • Leadership skills and volunteer experience
  • Demonstrated interest in Kepler
  • Valid passport and police clearance (international students only)

Checklist

Complete our online form to apply and be ready to provide the following information

  • Personal and contact information
  • Educational background, including secondary school scores
  • Brief responses about your interest and motivation for applying to Kepler
  • An active e-mail address
  • Copies of:
    • National ID or Passport
    • Ubudehe certificate (for Rwandans only)
    • S5 and S6 reports
    • National exam scores (if completed before this year)
